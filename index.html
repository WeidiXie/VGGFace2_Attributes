Additional VGGFace2 Attributes Annotation


Weidi Xie and Andrew Zisserman
Visual Geometry Group, University of Oxford


Abstract

VGGFace2 Attributes Dataset is a large-scale face attributes dataset with more than 30k celebrity images, each with annotations of 11 attributes. The images in this dataset cover large pose variations and background clutter.
